    I was a TA for the Master's level course taught by Henrik L. Pedersen. The course covers topics in Complex Analysis:
    • Infinite products
    • Weierstrass Factorization
    • Picard's Theorems
    • Conformal maps and Riemann Mapping Theorem
    • Harmonic and subharmonic functions
    • Phragmén-Lindelöf Theorem
    • Additional topics on entire functions
