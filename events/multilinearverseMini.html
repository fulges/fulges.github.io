MultilinearVerse Miniworkshop

MultilinearVerse is an online portal unifying the rapidly growing literature on tensors. It aims to serve as a guide through more than one century of algebra, geometry and applied mathematics, and provide the community with a resource to rapidly look up facts and track different relevant references.

The project started in 2024. The first MultilinearVerse Miniworkshop will take place in Trento, Italy, July 2-4, 2025, following the INABAG Conference 2025.

The goal is to discuss, gather opinions, brainstorm, and eventually contribute writing some mathematical content on the MultilinearVerse website. The participants are invited to read the MultilinearVerse contribution guidelines, and have a github account ready to use.

The workshop is open and free of charge. However, interested participants are invited to let the organizers Fulvio and Alessandro know about their intention to participate and contribute.
